Prim's algorithm

  1. Choose a cell randomly
  2. Mark this cell as part of the maze and add its walls to the wall list.
  3. Choose randomly a wall from the walllist and remove this wall from the wallist.
  4. If the neighbouring cells are both on the map or both are not on the map continue with step 3.
  5. If one is on the map and the orther not then put the latter one on the map.>
  6. then make a door in the wall and mark both cells as part of the maze.
  7. Add the other doors to the walllist. Continu with 3
