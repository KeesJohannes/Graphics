The Sierpinsky triangle is drawn by executing the Chaos Game

Sierpinsky drawing

Driving force: The Chaos Game: 


step 1: Generate a point within the triangle.

        This becomes the current point.

step 2: Draw the current point.

step 3: Calculate a new point halfway between the current point

        and a randomly choosen corner of the triangle.
 
        This calculated point becomes the new current point.

step 4: Repeat the steps 2,3 and 4 as many times as you like.
